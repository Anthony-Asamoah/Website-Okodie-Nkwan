About FKS


We are a reliable auto repair shop committed to delivering quality service for all types of vehicles.

Our team of skilled mechanics provides a full range of services including engine repairs, brake servicing, oil changes, and general vehicle maintenance. We use modern diagnostic tools to quickly identify and fix issues, saving you time and money. With a focus on honesty, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, we ensure every vehicle that comes through our shop leaves in excellent condition and ready for the road.
