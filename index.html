Our Story

About The Founder – Charity Appiah

A hardworking, resilient, and independent woman, Charity Appiah is a proud mother of two daughters who built her journey from humble beginnings to success through determination and perseverance.

The restaurant was founded as more than just a place to eat—it is a welcoming space where everyone can find comfort, good food, and a sense of community. Rooted in tradition, it serves as a refreshing stop for all who pass by, offering authentic Ghanaian flavours with warmth and hospitality.

Our Values

MISSION: To serve high-quality, authentic Ghanaian dishes with consistency and care, delivering excellent customer service while creating a welcoming space that feels like home.

VISION: To be recognized among the top restaurants in Ghana, celebrated for our rich flavours, cultural authenticity, and unforgettable dining experience.
